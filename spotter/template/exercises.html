PRO LEVEL GUIDANCE

Mastery & Intent

Tips

The Mechanics of Safety and Targeting

Neutral Spine

Keep your head, neck, and back in a straight line to prevent disc strain.

The Hip Hinge

Move from your hips, not your lower back; foundational for heavy pulls.

Joint Stacking

Keep wrists directly over elbows in presses to maximize force transfer.

Foot Rooting

"Screw" your feet into the floor to stabilize knees and create torque.

Full ROM

Move from full stretch to full contraction to recruit every muscle fiber.

Tricks

The Quality and Intensity of Effort

Mind-Muscle

Consciously "squeeze" the target muscle at the peak of the movement.

Explosive Power

Lift with maximum controlled speed; lower with absolute control.

Peak Pause

Hold the hardest part of the rep for 1 second to kill momentum.

Zero Momentum

If you have to swing, it's too heavy. Keep your torso locked.

Consistent Depth

Ensure the final rep reaches the same depth as the first rep.
