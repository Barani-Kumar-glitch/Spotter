PRO TIPS & TRICKS

{% for gym_game in gym_games|slice:":1" %}
01. REACTION TIME

Keep your fingers on the triggers; in {{ gym_game.lifting_sport }} games, timing is everything.

{% endfor %}
02. STAMINA MANAGEMENT

Don't burn your energy in the first round. Pace your clicks like your breath.

03. FORM FIRST

Perfect form in-game unlocks hidden multipliers. Watch the red bar.

04. MENTAL REP

Use Visualization. Before you touch the bar, close your eyes and "play" the entire lift in your head—from the grip to the final lockout.

05. EQUIPMENT LOADOUT

Use Chalk for grip and Lifting Shoes (with a hard, elevated heel) for better squat depth.

06. TACTICAL RECOVERY

Use Box Breathing (4 seconds in, 4 hold, 4 out, 4 hold) during your rest intervals.
